# Privacy Policy for Journey

**Effective Date: April 15, 2026**

Journey ("we", "us", or "our") is a private daily journal, notes, and planner application. We are committed to protecting your privacy while ensuring that your personal data remains strictly under your control.

## 1. Data Privacy (Local-First Architecture)
Journey is built as a **local-first** application. This means:
*   **Zero Server Storage**: 100% of your diary entries, notes, tasks, images, and PIN codes are stored **ONLY on your device**.
*   **We Do Not Collect Content**: We do not have servers for storing your content. We cannot read, access, or share your entries under any circumstances.
*   **No Personal Information**: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

## 2. Permissions Usage
To provide core functionality, Journey may request the following permissions:
*   **Biometrics/FaceID**: Used locally to unlock your journal securely.
*   **Photo Library**: Used only when you choose to attach images to your journal entries.
*   **Notifications**: Used to send optional daily reminders to write.
*   **Exact Alarm**: Used for scheduled task reminders.

## 3. Data Security
We use localized encryption (via Hive) to store your data on your device. Security features like PIN lock and Biometric authentication are processed locally and never transmitted externally.

## 4. User Responsibilities (Backups)
Because your data is exclusively on your device, deleting the app or clearing device storage will result in permanent data loss. We provide a **"Backup Data"** feature (JSON export) and **"PDF Export"** within the settings. We highly recommend using these features periodically.

## 5. Third-Party Services
*   **Google Fonts**: The app uses Google Fonts for typography. No personal data is shared with Google beyond standard font request metadata.
*   **Crashlytics/Analytics**: Non-identifiable crash logs provided by your device platform (Android/iOS) may be shared with us to help improve app stability.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our application does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
